The Hero Loop

  • Explore — Send scouts to discover anomalies, make first contact with alien civilizations
  • Expand — Colonize new systems, manage growth vs stability
  • Exploit — Trade with allies, spy on rivals, sabotage supply chains
  • Exterminate — War is inevitable. How you fight defines your legacy.

Controls

  • Click system to select it
  • Scroll to zoom, drag to pan the galaxy map
  • T toggle tech tree, D toggle diplomacy, H toggle help
  • Space end turn / advance phase

Ship Counters

Scout → countered by Fighters | Fighter → countered by Destroyers | Destroyer → countered by Battleships | Cruiser → countered by Bombers | Battleship → countered by Bombers & Swarm | Bomber → countered by Fighters/Destroyers | Carrier → countered by Bombers | Swarm → countered by Area damage (Bombers)

Terrain

  • Asteroid Fields: -20% accuracy, +30% Scout speed
  • Nebulae: -40% range, sensors disabled
  • Black Holes: +50% damage taken, ships can be pulled in
