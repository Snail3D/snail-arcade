Pieces
Tap a piece, then tap the grid to place it.

How to play

  • Pick one of the 3 pieces.
  • Tap the grid to place it.
  • Complete a full row or column to clear it.
  • Game ends when none of the pieces fit.

Keyboard: 1–3 selects a piece. Arrow keys move ghost preview. Enter places. Esc cancels selection.
