# PharmaSweep Game Plan

## Theme: Pharmacist Edition
Instead of mines, players must avoid "Critical Medication Errors" (e.g., Wrong Dose, Drug-Drug Interaction, Allergy Alert).
Instead of numbers, cells will indicate the number of "error risks" in adjacent cells.

## Mechanics
- **Grid**: 10x10 or 12x12 grid.
- **Mines (Errors)**: 15-20 hidden errors.
- **Cells**:
    - **Blank**: No errors nearby.
    - **Numbered (1-8)**: Number of adjacent errors.
    - **Flagged**: Player has identified a potential error.
- **Win Condition**: All safe cells (medications) are cleared without hitting an error.
- **Loss Condition**: Clicking an "Error" cell.

## UI/Visuals
- **Color Palette**: Medical blues, clean whites, and warning reds.
- **Icons**: Use 💊 (pill) for safe cells and ⚠️ (warning) for errors.
- **Theme**: A clean, clinical interface.
